# Important Disclaimer

## Not a Replacement for Emergency Services

Cordis is a **decision-support tool and research platform**. It is NOT a replacement for professional emergency dispatch systems, 911/112 services, or certified Computer Aided Dispatch (CAD) systems.

## Intended Use

Cordis is designed for:
- NGOs and humanitarian organizations coordinating disaster response
- Emergency management training and simulation exercises
- Campus safety and community response teams
- Hackathons and research on AI-assisted crisis coordination
- Municipal pilot programs for disaster preparedness
- Offline-capable crisis coordination in areas without infrastructure

## Not Intended For

Cordis should NOT be used as:
- A primary 911/112 emergency dispatch system
- A sole decision-maker for life-safety situations
- A replacement for certified medical triage protocols
- An autonomous dispatch system without human oversight

## Human Oversight Required

All dispatch recommendations made by Cordis require human review and approval before action. The system provides AI-assisted decision support — a human operator must always make the final call.

## Accuracy Limitations

- Intent classification uses ML models that can produce false positives/negatives
- Severity scoring is deterministic but calibrated for general use, not specific jurisdictions
- Logistics planning uses real geographic data but simplified cost/time models
- Emotion detection from text is inherently limited compared to voice analysis

## Liability

The authors and contributors of Cordis accept no liability for decisions made based on the system's output. Use at your own risk and always maintain human oversight for life-safety decisions.

## Regulatory Compliance

Deploying Cordis in a production emergency response context may require:
- Local regulatory approval
- HIPAA compliance (for medical data in the US)
- GDPR compliance (for personal data in the EU)
- Certification by relevant emergency management authorities
- Privacy impact assessments
- Data retention policies

Consult legal counsel before deploying in any regulated environment.
